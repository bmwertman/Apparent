What is Apparent?

Apparent is a communication platform that, through parent organizations, helps parents be engaged in their child(ren)’s school. They use the Apparent App or the Apparent Website - if multiple devices are used, they will all sync with each other.

Parents access and set up an account on Apparent by signing up by providing and verifying their email and setting a password.

School Leaders also play a meaningful role on Apparent. They can share notes, photos, and videos. Only teachers, parents, and students connected to classes in the school can see these posts. School Leaders can also create classes, share classes with teachers to give feedback to students and message with parents, plus see Highlights - a high-level overview of all the feedback and engagement happening within their school.

More information on how the Service operates is located here and your participation and use of the Service is governed by our Terms of Service (“Terms”).

Apparent may send push notifications to Parents or School Leaders - for example, telling them about an achievement milestone they reached, upcoming events or for event support outreach.
